Outsiders in brazillian politics
Advisor: Adriano Codato

In Brazil data on political candidatures are provided by the Supreme Electoral Court. It's messy and scattered over more than 300 .txt and .csv files.

This is also an academic research project where we attempt to identify party candidate launching behaviours and also voting behaviour. It's just starting, and you can find more about it in my github page.
